The Art Of Dungeon Diving

This webpage will give you a better understanding of how to complete the Destiny2 Dungeon "Ghost Of The Deep".

So you're new to the game and you want ot tackle one of the best parts of the grind experience. The most important thing is to have information on the boss type and their weakness and strengths. for example in Ghost Of The Deep the second encounter is a hive knight those are weak to swords, he also has a powerful over shield that needs to be removed quickly. The best weapon I have found for this boss is the exotic sword The Lament, it quickly removes his barrier and deals a substantial amount of damage to hive.

For the last boss its important to learn the puzzle to get her to damage phase it's much more complicated than the previous puzzles. once you understand how to get her into that damage phase oyu need to know the best way to burn her health down as fast as possible. the Lament is rather useless in this engagement so you need to change your strategy completely, the key to your success is The Arbalest an exotic linear fusion rifle that removes the shield immediately. you will also need a high dps heavy linear fusion rifle if you have a team then Whisper Of The Worm is a great heavy to carry into this fight. This boss never touches the ground so make sure all equipped supers can reach her and all your weapons are mid-long range.

KEY POINTS

  • Learn all the puzzles before attempting the dungeon
  • Bring the right weapons to the right fight
  • Remove the over shield before burning all your ammo
  • Remember to have fun and don't worry if you die a few times
